What's Included

A comprehensive onboarding experience

Every element is designed to help new hires feel welcomed, clear, and connected from their first day forward.

01 A Structured Onboarding Plan

A clear, step-by-step framework that outlines what happens before day one, during the first week, and throughout the first 90 days. No guesswork, just clarity.

02 Gen Z Communication & Support Tools

Practical resources that help managers communicate in ways that resonate with early-career talent. Templates, conversation guides, and feedback frameworks included.

03 Manager Alignment Sessions

Working sessions with managers to ensure everyone understands their role in the onboarding experience and feels equipped to support new hires.

04 30, 60, and 90 Day Onboarding Roadmap

A milestone-based plan that tracks progress, sets clear expectations, and creates natural check-in points throughout the onboarding journey.

05 Clarity Around Expectations & Feedback Cycles

Frameworks for setting expectations early and establishing feedback rhythms that help new hires understand where they stand and how to grow.
